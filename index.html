THE BAND

We love music!

Greg Warren Band is a young, high energy country band based out of Orlando Florida. Formed in 2011, their very first show was headlining the House of Blues in Orlando with support from Jared Blake (The Voice). Since then the band has since gone on to support national acts Hunter Hayes, Alan Jackson, Hank Williams Jr, Scotty McCreery,  Gary Allen, Chris Cagle, Thompson Square, Love and Theft, Eli Young Band, Little Texas and more.
